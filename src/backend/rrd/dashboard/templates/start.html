About Our Initiative

The Hazard and Climate Resilience Institute designs and conducts cross-sector community resilience assessments for rural communities.

Our Mission

Helping rural communities understand risks, implement mitigation strategies, and recover/plan for the future.

Our Vision

Creating a network of resilient rural communities prepared for changing environmental and economic challenges.

Why Resilience Matters

Rural communities face unique challenges that require specialized resilience strategies.

Geographic Isolation

Rural areas face longer emergency response times and limited resources.

Limited Resources

Smaller budgets require strategic resilience planning.

Aging Infrastructure

Many rural areas need upgrades to withstand extreme events.

Economic Vulnerability

Rural economies are often dependent on a single industry.

Demographic Changes

Many rural communities face population decline and aging residents, creating unique challenges for resilience planning.

Climate Impacts

Rural communities often depend on natural resources and agriculture, making them particularly vulnerable to climate change impacts.
